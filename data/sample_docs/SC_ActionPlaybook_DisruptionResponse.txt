Disruption Response Playbook (Synthetic)
When a disruption is detected for priority materials:
1) Activate war-room within 24h (Ops + Procurement + Logistics).
2) Validate on-hand + in-transit coverage (days of supply).
3) Decide: expedite vs replan vs alternate source.
4) Communicate to customer success with updated ETA within 48h.
5) Post-incident review within 7 days; update risk register and SOP.
