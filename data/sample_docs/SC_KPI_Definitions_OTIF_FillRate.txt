Supply Chain KPI Definitions (v1.3)
- OTIF (On-Time In-Full): percentage of customer orders delivered on or before requested delivery date AND with complete quantities.
- Target OTIF for Priority SKUs: 95%.
- Fill Rate: shipped units / ordered units per order line.
- Expedite Rate: percent of shipments requiring premium freight (air/express) due to late supply.
Notes: OTIF is reported weekly; expedite cost is tracked monthly by lane and supplier.
